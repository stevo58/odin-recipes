Tacos

What are tacos

A taco is sort of like a burrito but way smaller! They typically use corn tortillas instead of the large flour tortillas that burritos use. similarly to burritos tacos are filled with yummy ingredients but instead of rolling them up like a burrito you just eat them by folding the taco itself.

Ingredients

  • prepared carne asada steak
  • 1-2 avocados
  • 12 (4-inch) corn tortillas
  • ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 jalapeno pepper sliced
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup crumbled cotija cheese
  • Lime wedges optional, for serving

Steps

  1. Grill the carne asada and cook according to the recipe instructions.
  2. Slice the carne asada
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork. Add in a sprinkle of salt and pepper and a squeeze of lime juice. Stir.
  4. Fill each tortilla with a spread of mashed avocado and a scoop of meat.
  5. Top the tacos with fresh cilantro, jalapeños, onion, and cotija cheese. Finish with a squeeze of lime juice over the top.
